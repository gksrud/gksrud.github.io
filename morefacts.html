Funny facts about Jamie Oliver

  • When he was 38, he finished reading a book for the first time.
  • He thinks women are better at BBQ than men.
  • He is England's second best-selling author, after JK Rowling (author of the Harry Potter series).
  • He secretly collects driftwood. "It might sound a bit mad, but a solid bit of driftwood makes for a perfect chopping board, the kind you'd pay a small fortune for in a department store."
  • Although he frequently shares pictures of his family on social media, Jamie Oliver has banned his children Poppy, Daisy, Petal and Buddy from having their own Instagram accounts or cell phones.
  • When he was ten, he released a stink bomb in his parents' Essex pub, The Cricketers, causing all the customers to flee.
  • One of his early successes was catering for then-Prime Minister Tony Blair at an Anglo-Italian summit. Blair reportedly asked for a doggie bag.
  • He admitted on Instagram that when he visits Toronto, all he really wants to do is "pimp the CN Tower in to a giant Doner Kebab".
